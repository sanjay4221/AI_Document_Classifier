Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when registering (name, email, password) and documents you upload for classification. Document content is processed temporarily for classification purposes only.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide document classification services
  • To maintain your account and classification history
  • To improve classification accuracy over time

3. Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely in Neon PostgreSQL hosted in AWS Asia Pacific (Sydney). Uploaded PDFs are stored temporarily and can be deleted on request.

4. Third Party Services

We use Groq API to process document text for AI classification. Document text is sent to Groq's servers for inference. Groq's privacy policy applies to this processing.
